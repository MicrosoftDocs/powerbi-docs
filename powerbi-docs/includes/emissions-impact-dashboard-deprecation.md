> **Emissions Impact Dashboard retirement notice**
>
> Effective March 31, 2027, the Emissions Impact Dashboard for Azure hosted by Power BI will be retired. After this date, the dashboard will no longer be accessible, and technical support will be discontinued.
>
> We strongly encourage you to export your emissions data to retain historical reporting. Consider Azure Carbon Optimizer as an alternative solution before the retirement date. For more information, see [*Carbon optimization in Azure*](/azure/carbon-optimization/). To provide feedback, visit [*Azure Carbon Optimization - Community*](https://feedback.azure.com/d365community/forum/1694d59b-a692-ee11-be37-00224827362a).
>
> This article will be retained for reference until March 31, 2027, and will not be updated further.
