The in-report Copilot in the Power BI mobile apps provides a full conversational chat experience for the report you're currently viewing. You can ask custom questions, generate summaries, and explore insights using natural language. Access Copilot directly from any open report by tapping the Copilot icon in the report header. This helps you make data-driven decisions on the go, anytime, anywhere, without spending time analyzing the data yourself.

> [!TIP]
> Looking for the standalone Copilot that works across all your Power BI content? See [Standalone Copilot in Power BI mobile apps (preview)](mobile-standalone-copilot.md) to learn about the full-screen Copilot experience accessible from the app's home page.

This article shows you how to use the in-report Copilot feature. The images illustrate the feature as it appears on phones. The experience on iPads and Android tablets is similar.

## Use Copilot to chat with your report data

1. Open the report you're interested in and tap the **Copilot** icon.

   :::image type="content" source="./media/mobile-apps-copilot/copilot-mobile-chat-open.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Copilot icon in a report header." border="false":::

   > [!NOTE]
   > You can use Copilot for any report that meets [Copilot for Power BI requirements](../../create-reports/copilot-introduction.md). If you don't see the Copilot icon in the report header, it probably means that the report doesn't meet these requirements.
   >
   > Copilot works on the current state of the report, so be sure to apply any filters or slicers before you tap the Copilot button.

1. In the chat window that opens, type your own question, or choose from the prompt gallery to get started.

   :::image type="content" source="./media/mobile-apps-copilot/copilot-mobile-chat-prompt.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the Copilot chat window with prompt options." border="false":::

1. Copilot generates its response, which can include both text and interactive visuals. You can enlarge the chat window to show more of the response if necessary.

   Keep chatting with your data — ask follow-up questions to refine your queries and dive deeper into report insights. If you want to return to the report, close the Copilot pane by swiping it down. The chat history stays intact until the report is refreshed or closed, so unless that happens, you can see all the prompts and responses again by tapping the Copilot button.

   The following image shows a response in the Copilot chat window (in its enlarged state) that includes a visual along with the text, and citations for the visuals used to generate the answer. You have controls to copy the response, read aloud, and provide feedback.

   :::image type="content" source="./media/mobile-apps-copilot/copilot-mobile-chat-answer.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the features of the Copilot in Power BI Mobile chat pane." border="false":::

## Explore Copilot's results

When you ask about a metric or KPI, Copilot often includes a visualization along with its text response. Tap the visual to open a pane where you can interact with it and explore the details. The visual is interactive, so you can dive into it deeper, long-tapping for tooltips, drilling down or up, and more.

When you ask Copilot to summarize a report, the response includes a summary with relevant citations. Tap a citation to open the visual in focus mode, where you can view and interact with it.

## Use voice input (iPhone and iPad)

On iPhone and iPad, the in-report Copilot supports dictation, so you can ask hands-free questions. Use **Read aloud** to listen to the response.

## Share Copilot's results with others

To share Copilot's response with others, tap the **Copy** icon at the top of response or tap **Share** from the visual pane. The response is copied to the clipboard, and you can then paste it into an email, Teams chat, or any other platform to share it with colleagues. The copied response includes a link to the report so that recipients can easily refer to the report.
