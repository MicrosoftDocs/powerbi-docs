# Power BI video playlists

Power BI videos are organized into topic playlists on YouTube and featured in the Community video gallery. [Subscribe to the Power BI video channel](https://www.youtube.com/@MicrosoftPowerBI/videos) to get notified when new videos are added.

> [!VIDEO https://www.youtube.com/embed/9rh_qSK-UKQ]

> [!NOTE]
> Videos might use earlier versions of Power BI Desktop or the Power BI service.

## Get started

New to Power BI? These videos introduce core concepts and walk you through the basics.

- [Power BI guided learning series](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL1N57mwBHtN0JFoKSR0n-tBkUJHeMP2cP) — a structured playlist for new users, from sign-in to your first report
- [What is Power BI?](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yKTSLffVGbk) — a short overview of the product
- [Power BI Desktop tutorials](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL1N57mwBHtN2qCeClkCEoWQqqFwJPGnPt) — hands-on walkthroughs for building reports in Desktop

Not sure where to start? [Find your learning path](power-bi-learning-path-directory.md) to get a curated sequence of docs for your role.
