# Before you contact Power BI and Fabric support

Before you create a support request, use the following checklist to try to resolve your issue and gather the information that Microsoft Support needs to help you.

## Pre-support checklist

1. **Check for service outages or degradation.** Visit the [Fabric support page](https://support.fabric.microsoft.com) to see whether there's an active incident affecting your service. You can also [track service health in the Microsoft 365 admin center](service-admin-health.md) for current and historical issue details.

1. **Review current known issues.** Check the [known issues for Fabric and Power BI](https://support.fabric.microsoft.com/known-issues) to see if your problem has already been identified and is being worked on.

1. **Search the community.** Post or search the [Fabric & Power BI Community](https://community.powerbi.com) to see if other users have encountered and resolved the same issue.

1. **Check the planned maintenance schedule.** Your issue might be related to [planned maintenance](service-planned-maintenance.md). Review the schedule to rule out a maintenance window.

1. **Try to resolve sign-in issues.** If you can't sign in, see [Resolve sign-in issues for Power BI and Fabric](power-bi-cannot-sign-in.md) for password reset options and other solutions.

1. **Find your organization and region details.** You might need to provide your organization ID, name, or default region when you create a support ticket. Use these resources to look up that information:
   - [Find your organization ID and name](/power-platform/admin/determine-org-id-name)
   - [Find the default region for your organization](../admin/service-admin-where-is-my-tenant-located.md)

1. **Capture diagnostic information.** Collect browser traces and other [diagnostic information](service-admin-capturing-additional-diagnostic-information-for-power-bi.md) to attach to your support request. Having this information ready can help speed up resolution.

If you can't resolve your issue with these steps, [create a support ticket](create-support-ticket.md).
