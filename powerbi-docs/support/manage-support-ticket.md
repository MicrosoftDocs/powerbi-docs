# Manage a support ticket in Fabric

After you [create a Power BI or a Fabric support ticket](create-support-ticket.md), you can manage it in the [Fabric admin portal](https://app.powerbi.com/admin-portal/supportCenter). From there, you can view ticket details and status, send messages to Microsoft support, update contact information, allow the collection of advanced diagnostic information, and close resolved tickets.

## View support tickets

View the details and status of support tickets by going to **Help + Support** in the Fabric admin portal. You can review all the support tickets that you created or that list you as a secondary owner.

:::image type="content" source="media/manage-support-ticket/help-support-admin-portal.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the left navigation in the Admin Portal with Help+Support highlighted.":::

You can search, filter, and sort support tickets. By default, you see only tickets that you created. Change the filter options to review support tickets where you're a secondary owner. You can also filter tickets by who owns the pending response: Microsoft or you.

To view a support ticket's details, including its severity, contacts, and the last messages associated with the ticket, select it from the list.

Admins can review all the support tickets in their organization.

> [!NOTE]
> You can generally view closed support tickets for 13 months. After that time, they might be removed, making them unavailable to view.

## Send a message to Microsoft support

You can communicate with Microsoft support directly from the ticket details view.

1. Select the support ticket, and then select **Details**.

1. Select **Communication history** in the support ticket details view.

1. Enter your message, and then select **Send message**.

## Allow collection of advanced diagnostic information

When you create a support ticket, you can choose **Allow** or **Not allow** access to your advanced diagnostic information. This option determines whether Microsoft support can access advanced telemetry information that might help resolve your issue.

To change your **Advanced diagnostic information** selection after the support ticket is created:

1. Select the support ticket, and then select **Edit**.

1. Select the consent option, and then select **Save**.

For more information, see [Consent to access customer data](access-customer-data.md).

:::image type="content" source="media/manage-support-ticket/consent-to-access-data.png" alt-text="Screenshot of the consent change option in ticket's details.":::

## Update support ticket contact information

You can change the contact details and add or remove secondary owners on an existing support ticket.

1. Select the support ticket, and then select **Edit**.

1. Update the contact information details.

1. Add or remove secondary owners on the support ticket.

1. Select **Save**.

Only the ticket owner and the admin can update the support ticket owner's contact information. Any owner can update the secondary owners' list.

## Close a support ticket

To close a support ticket, select the support ticket, and then select **Details**. Scroll down in either the **Details** or **Communication history** tab, and then select **Close support ticket**. When prompted to confirm, select **Close**. You receive a confirmation email when your ticket is closed.

Only the ticket owner and the admin can close the support ticket.
