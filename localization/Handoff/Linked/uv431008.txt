	<title>Share (and unshare) a dashboard from Power BI</title>	
	
	<p><span style="line-height: 1.4em;">You can share your dashboards with people in your organization.&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 1.4em;">If they haven't signed up for <a href="http://powerbi.com">Power BI</a>, they'll need to&nbsp;do so to see your dashboard.</span></p><ol><li>&nbsp;Open the dashboard to share and click <b>Share Dashboard</b>.<br><br><span><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/82600012/sharedash.png"></span><span><br><br></span></li><li><span><span>To share with coworkers, click <b>Inï»¿vite </b>and type the coworkers' email addresses in the top box and, optionally,&nbsp;modify the message. <br><br></span></span></li><li><span><span>To allow your coworkers to reshare your dashboard with others, check <b>Allow recipients to share your dashboard </b>(the checkbox is in the lower-right corner).&nbsp;<br><br><span><img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/79790010/PBI_ShareInvite.png"></span><br><br></span></span></li><li>Click <b>Share.</b></li><p></p><p>Your coworkers will&nbsp;receive an email invitation with a link to the shared dashboard. Clicking the link adds the dashboard to their Power BI. If they haven't yet signed up for Power BI, the email includes instructions to do so.<br></p>

<br><li>To see who the dashboard is shared with, click <b>Shared With</b>.

<h2>Notes about sharing</h2><br>When you share with your colleagues:<br><ul style="font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;"><li>They can see your dashboard and interact with your reports in&nbsp;<a href="http://support.powerbi.com/knowledgebase/articles/439920-open-a-report-in-reading-view">Reading View</a>.&nbsp;They can't create new reports or save changes to existing reports.</li><li>They can even see the changes you make (after you save those changes).&nbsp;</li><li>They can't see or download the dataset or use any of the data refresh operations.</li><li>They see this icon&nbsp;<img src="https://powerbi.uservoice.com/assets/77650908/PBI_SharedWithYouIcon.png">&nbsp;next to the shared dashboard, indicating the dashboard is shared with them.</li></ul><span style="line-height: 19.6px; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">You can share with users who have the same email domain as you, and with users whose domain is different but registered within the tenant. For example, say the domains contoso.com and contoso2.com are registered in the tenant.&nbsp;</span><span style="line-height: 1.4em; font-family: sans-serif; font-size: 14px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">If your email address is konrads@contoso.com, you can share with ravali@contoso.com, and also with gustav@contoso2.com.<br></span><br><b>TIP: </b>If your colleagues already have access to a specific dashboard, you can send a direct link to that dashboard just by copying the URL when you're on the dashboard. For example:<br><ul><li>https://powerbi.com/dashboards/g12466b5-a452-4e55-8634-xxxxxxxxxxxx</li></ul><span style="font-size: 22.39px;"><b>Resharing</b></span><p>Resharing allows your coworkers to forward the email invitation to others in your organization (the invitation expires after one month). Coworkers can also reshare through the web and mobile apps. As the owner of the dashboard, you can turn off resharing and you can also revoke resharing on an individual basis (see below).<br></p><h1>Unshare a dashboard<br></h1>
